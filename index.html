01
About CampusLink

Built for how campuses actually move.

Campus commerce already happens — in hostel WhatsApp groups, gate-side stalls, and bike riders who know every shortcut. CampusLink doesn't replace that energy; it gives it infrastructure.

One platform lets a student order from a verified seller down the road, track who's bringing it, and have it land at their hostel gate — handled by a fellow student who knows the campus better than any outside courier ever could.

It's commerce and logistics, scoped to a world that's dense, fast-moving, and currently underserved by every general delivery app built for cities, not campuses.

02
The problem

Campus commerce runs on goodwill, not infrastructure.

Four gaps show up again and again when commerce has to route through general-purpose apps that were never built for hostel blocks and lecture-hall foot traffic.

⏱

Slow, generic delivery

City-wide delivery apps route through addresses that don't match how campuses are actually laid out — hostels, faculties, gates.

🛡

Unverified sellers

No reliable way to know who's actually behind a stall or a DM-based shop until money has already changed hands.

💳

Payment trust gaps

Pay-before-you-see-it arrangements over chat leave both buyers and sellers exposed, with no record to fall back on.

🧩

Fragmented WhatsApp commerce

Every seller has their own group, their own process — there's no single place to discover what's actually available nearby.

Verification & KYC Setup Guide

Step 01

Choose Your Niche

Launch the portal, click "Join CampusLink", and pick the Seller role to start configuring your store profile.

Step 02

Upload General Details

Provide your fundamental parameters including business name, active phone number, and targeted campus hub location.

Step 03

KYC & Inventory Setup

Upload a clean identification passport photo alongside crisp images showcasing the catalog of items you intend to retail.

Step 04

Generate Registration Slip

Once your profile matrix is complete, review your data ecosystem and trigger the instant download of your official Registration Slip.

Step 05

Email Submission Routing

Launch your email terminal, address it to the onboarding node, and ensure you explicitly attach the downloaded slip.

Step 06

Verification Hold Matrix

Your documentation is safely logged. Keep an active eye on your inbox for our official clearance and portal deployment confirmation code.
